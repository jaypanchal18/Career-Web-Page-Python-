Flutter Developer

DESCRIPTION:

We are seeking a skilled Flutter Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing high-quality applications. If you're passionate about mobile development and eager to work on cutting-edge projects, this role offers an exciting opportunity to grow your career.

About the Role:

Develop and maintain high-quality mobile applications using Flutter framework. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features. Work on bug fixing and improving application performance. Continuously discover, evaluate, and implement new technologies to maximize development efficiency.

  1. Proven experience (10 years) as a mobile developer with expertise in Flutter.
  2. Strong understanding of Dart programming language and Flutter framework..
  3. Experience with third-party libraries and APIs.
  4. Ability to work independently and as part of a collaborative team environment.
  5. Excellent communication skills and ability to articulate technical concepts effectively.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • 10 years of experience in mobile application development.
  • Proficiency in Dart and Flutter framework.
  • Experience with version control (Git, SVN, etc.).
  • Strong portfolio of previous mobile applications developed using Flutter.
