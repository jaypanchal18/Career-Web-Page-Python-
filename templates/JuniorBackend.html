Junior-Backend

Job ID: 2511698

DESCRIPTION:

We're on the lookout for a Junior Backend Developer to join our team and contribute to the development of robust server-side applications. This role offers an excellent opportunity to work alongside experienced developers, learn from them, and actively participate in building scalable and efficient backend solutions.

About the Role:

Collaborate with senior developers to design, develop, and maintain backend services and applications. Assist in coding, testing, and debugging backend systems to ensure functionality and optimization. Learn and apply best practices in backend development, working with languages like Python, Java, Node.js, etc. Work closely with frontend developers to integrate user-facing elements with server-side logic. Participate in code reviews, discussions on architecture, and contribute innovative ideas to projects.

Expectations/Requirements:

  1. Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering
  2. Understanding of databases (SQL and/or NoSQL) and data modeling concepts.
  3. SStrong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  4. Familiarity with software testing principles and frameworks.
  5. EGood communication skills and ability to work effectively within a team.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • 5+ years of experience as a Software Engineer or similar role.
  • Familiarity with backend programming languages (e.g., Python, Java, Node.js, etc.)
  • Familiarity with software testing principles and frameworks.
