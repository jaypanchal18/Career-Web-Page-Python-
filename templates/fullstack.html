Full Stack Developer

Job ID: 2505

DESCRIPTION:

We are seeking a talented Full Stack Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing and maintaining both front-end and back-end applications, collaborating with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features, and continuously discovering and implementing new technologies to maximize development efficiency.

About the Role:

Develop and maintain scalable, secure, and high-performance web applications. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define project requirements and deliver features. Design user interactions on web pages, ensuring responsiveness and a seamless user experience. Troubleshoot and debug applications to optimize performance. Stay updated with emerging technologies and industry trends to continuously improve our development processes.

Expectations/Requirements:

  1. Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience).
  2. Proven experience as a Full Stack Developer or similar role.
  3. Proficiency in front-end technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern JS frameworks/libraries (e.g., React, Angular, Vue.js).
  4. Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
  5. Familiarity with databases (SQL and NoSQL), web servers, and RESTful APIs.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • 5+ years years of experience as a Full Stack Developer or similar role.
  • Experience with back-end development using languages like Node.js, Python, or Java.
  • Familiarity with databases (SQL and NoSQL), web servers, and RESTful APIs.
