Ad Sales Manager

Job ID: 123432

DESCRIPTION:

We are seeking an experienced and motivated Ad Sales Manager to join our team. In this role, you will be responsible for developing and implementing strategic advertising sales plans, fostering client relationships, and driving revenue growth through advertising sales. The ideal candidate should have a proven track record in sales, excellent communication skills, and a deep understanding of the advertising industry.

About the Role:

Develop and execute strategic plans to achieve sales targets and expand our client base. Identify new business opportunities and partnerships to maximize revenue. Build and maintain strong relationships with clients, agencies, and advertising partners. Collaborate with the marketing team to create compelling sales pitches and proposals. Monitor industry trends and competitor activities to identify market opportunities.

Expectations/Requirements:

  1. Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business Administration, or related field.
  2. Proven experience in advertising sales, with a minimum of 5 years in a similar role.
  3. Strong negotiation and presentation skills.
  4. Familiarity with CRM software and sales analytics tools.
  5. Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business Administration, or related field.
  • Proven track record of meeting and exceeding sales targets.
  • Strong understanding of advertising principles and industry trends.
