Android Developer

Job ID: 2511541

DESCRIPTION:

We are looking for a skilled Android Developer to join our innovative team. This role presents an exciting opportunity for a passionate developer to contribute to the creation of cutting-edge mobile applications. If you thrive in a fast-paced environment and are dedicated to crafting top-notch user experiences, we'd love to have you on board.

About the Role:

Design and build advanced applications for the Android platform. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features. Continuously discover, evaluate, and implement new technologies to maximize development efficiency. Work on bug fixing and improving application performance. Maintain code quality, organization, and automation. Participate in code reviews, discussions on architecture, and contribute innovative ideas to projects.

Expectations/Requirements:

  1. Proven experience (10 years) as an Android Developer with a strong portfolio.
  2. Proficiency in Java/Kotlin programming languages and Android SDK.
  3. Familiarity with RESTful APIs to connect applications to back-end services.
  4. Solid understanding of the full mobile development life cycle.
  5. Ability to collaborate effectively in a team environment and independently when needed.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or relevant field.
  • 10 years of experience in Android application development.
  • Proficiency in Java or Kotlin and Android SDK..
