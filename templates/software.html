Sr Events Manager, India Consumables

Job ID: 2505970 | Location - USA only

DESCRIPTION:

We are looking for a talented, smart and entrepreneurial Marketing Manager who will be responsible for defining our events strategy, will own the events calendar and will drive awareness and customer participation across these programs.

About the Role:

In this role you will work directly with teams across JobTech to build the right go-to-market strategy, develop key marketing inputs and handle end to end execution of the events. The Events Manager is also responsible for the design, management and ongoing optimization of our daily deals program and also monitor & scale them during large scale events, including Diwali and Prime Day.

Expectations/Requirements:

  1. 5+ years of professional non-internship marketing/category experience
  2. Experience with Excel or Tableau (data manipulation, macros, charts and pivot tables)
  3. Experience building, executing and scaling cross-functional marketing programs
  4. Experience driving direction and alignment with cross-functional teams
  5. Experience communicating results to stakeholders

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • 5+ years of professional non-internship marketing experience.
  • Experience using data and metrics to drive improvements
  • Experience developing and executing campaigns across a multitude of timezones and languages
