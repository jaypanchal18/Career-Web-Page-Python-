Junior Frontend Developer

Job ID: 111232

DESCRIPTION:

We're seeking a motivated Junior Frontend Developer to join our team. This role offers an exciting opportunity to learn and grow in a dynamic environment while contributing to the development of cutting-edge web applications. The ideal candidate will work closely with our senior developers to create engaging user experiences and scalable frontend solutions.

About the Role:

Collaborate with senior developers to craft responsive and visually appealing web applications. Assist in the design and implementation of user-facing features. Learn and apply best practices in frontend development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Collaborate with design teams to translate wireframes and mockups into functional web pages. Contribute to code reviews and participate in team discussions on architecture and best practices.

Expectations/Requirements:

  1. Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering
  2. Strong understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  3. Familiarity with frontend frameworks/libraries (e.g., React, Angular, Vue.js).
  4. Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  5. Good communication skills and ability to work collaboratively in a team.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Basic understanding of version control systems like Git.
